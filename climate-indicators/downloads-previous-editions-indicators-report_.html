Downloads for Previous Editions of the Indicators Report

EPA has published a new printed edition of Climate Change Indicators in the United States every two years, starting in 2010. This page provides access to previous editions, along with related fact sheets and brochures featuring content from the report. All of these publications are available below as downloadable PDF files.

  • Printer-friendly PDF versions of each indicator (containing all text and figures)
  • Detailed technical documentation for EPA's indicators
  • High-resolution figures and the numerical data underlying the figures (on each indicator page)
