Communicating Vulnerabilities to Climate Change: Indigenous Populations

EPA has developed communication materials that summarize key points from the U.S. Climate and Health Assessment on eight different populations that are disproportionately affected by climate change impacts. Stakeholders are encouraged to use or modify these materials to strengthen their conversations about climate change and health impacts to vulnerable populations.
