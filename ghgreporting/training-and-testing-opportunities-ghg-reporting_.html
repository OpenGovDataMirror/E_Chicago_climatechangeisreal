Previous Webinars

GHGRP: How to Use FLIGHT to View Reporting Year 2015 Data. These webinars were held on October 5 and October 13. With the release of the Reporting Year 2015 data, EPA hosted technical webinars to showcase the Facility Level Information on Greenhouse Gases Tool (FLIGHT), demonstrate common searches, show how to do custom queries, and highlight new features added this year. Other GHGRP data summaries were also highlighted.

GHGRP Subparts L & OO – Reporting Year 2015 Overview. This webinar was held Tuesday, March 1st, from 12:00 to 1:00pm EST. EPA discussed updates to reporting GHG data for RY2015 under Subpart L, Fluorinated Gas Production and Subpart OO, Suppliers of Industrial Gases. This webinar covered the reporting requirements for RY2015 and modifications to the reporting forms for facilities that report under Subpart L and for producers of industrial gases that report under Subpart OO. View slides from the webinar.

GHGRP Subpart W - Reporting Year 2015 Overview. This webinar was held Tuesday, February 23rd, from 3:00 to 4:00pm EST. EPA provided information regarding updates to reporting GHG data for RY2015 under Subpart W, Petroleum and Natural Gas Systems. This webinar reviewed modifications to the Subpart W reporting form for RY15. View slides from the webinar.

e-GGRT: Reporting Year 2015 Overview. This webinar was held Thursday, February 18th, from 1:00 to 2:00pm EST. EPA provided general updates on reporting GHG data for RY2015. An additional presentation of this webinar was held Thursday, February 23rd, from 3:00 to 4:00pm EST. View slides from the webinar.

Subpart W - New Requirements for Reporting Year 2016. This webinar was held Wednesday, January 20, from 2:00-3:00pm EST. EPA provided an overview of the new Subpart W (Petroleum and Natural Gas Systems) requirements for reporting year 2016, including the new Onshore Petroleum and Natural Gas Gathering and Boosting and Onshore Natural Gas Transmission Pipelines segments and the inclusion of well identification numbers and oil wells with hydraulic fracturing in the existing Onshore Petroleum and Natural Gas Production segment. View slides from the webinar.
